<h3>Burpee Tutorial</h3><br>
Begin in a standing position.
1.Move into a squat position with your hands on the ground. (count 1)<br>
2.Kick your feet back into a plank position, while keeping your arms extended. (count 2)<br>
3.Immediately return your feet into squat position. (count 3)<br>
4.Stand up from the squat position (count 4)<br>
