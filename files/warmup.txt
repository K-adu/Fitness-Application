<h3>Warm up</h3>
<h6>Follow this three-step method to warm up wisely.</h6>
Walk. Walk gently for three to five minutes. 
Add strides. Do five to six 100-meter strides.
Do dynamic stretches. Static stretching, where you hold a muscle in an elongated, <br>
fixed position for 30 seconds or more, is now discouraged prerun, as it's been linked to injury.
