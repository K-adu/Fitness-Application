<h3>Push-ups tutorial</h3>
To do a push-up, get into a face-down prone position. <br>
Keep your feet together. Place your hands near your shoulders,<br>
 elbows pointed out and back at 45 degrees. ... <br>
<h6>Stance:</h6> Hands on the floor in a line, feet together. 
<h6>Toes:</h6> Both feet touching each other.
